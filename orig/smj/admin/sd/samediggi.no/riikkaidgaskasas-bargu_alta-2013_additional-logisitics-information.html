Additional logisitics information

04. ruffie 2013

The Sámi Parliament in Norway is hosting a Global Indigenous Preparatory Conference for the World Conference on Indigenous Peoples from 10 - 12 June 2013 in Alta, Norway.

For more information about this Preparatory Conference and the World Conference on Indigenous Peoples 2014 itself, please see: www.wcip2014.org.

Always check the weather

Even if the weather is fine in the morning it does not mean that it will stay that way all day.

Check the weather forecast: http://www.yr.no

The forecast says that it will be some light rain and approximately 10 degrees Celsius. We still recommend you to bring sunscreen, as the weather might shift. We also recommend you to bring some light mosquito spray, a rain jacket, a warm sweater and a good pair of shoes.

Taxi

There are taxis available in Alta, and drivers usually speak English.

You can hail a taxi off the street or pick one up at the many taxi ranks throughout the cities and towns - or simply phone for one yourself. + 47 784 35 353

All taxis in Norway accept the most common credit cards such as VISA, American Express, Diners Club, Eurocard and MasterCard. You should inform the driver at the start of the trip that payment will be done with a credit card.

Food/dining

Breakfast and dinner is served at your hotel. Please ask the staff at your hotel when breakfast and dinner is served.  If you choose to dine other places Alta has several restaurants in the town center.

Lunch is served at the Conference Hall between 1:00 and 3:00pm.

Media centre

A dedicated media centre will be established, with wireless network access, workstations and direct feeds from the conference centre. Press pass-holders also have access to reserved seating in the conference hall itself. The media centre is open from Sunday, 9 June at 12.00am, then every day of the conference between 8.00am and 6.00pm.
